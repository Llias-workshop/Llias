Workshop Overview

LLAIS: Workshop on LLM-Based Agents for Intelligent Systems

The LLAIS Workshop at ECAI explores the transformative role of LLM-based agents in advancing intelligent systems. LLMs have revolutionized AI-driven applications, yet their full potential remains untapped in domains such as retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), knowledge graphs, information retrieval, software automation, reproducibility in research, and next-generation recommender systems.

This workshop serves as a bridge between researchers, industry professionals, and practitioners, providing a platform to discuss innovations, challenges, and opportunities in developing and deploying LLM-powered intelligent agents. Through interactive sessions, keynotes from leading AI experts, and demo presentations, LLAIS will facilitate cutting-edge discussions on enhancing AI adaptability, reasoning, and decision-making capabilities across diverse domains.

Key Topics

  • Knowledge Graph Enhancement
    • Automated Knowledge Graph Construction
    • Enhanced Knowledge Graph Completion
    • Improved Knowledge Graph Querying and Reasoning
  • Developing Next-Generation Recommender Systems
    • Enhanced User Understanding
    • Creative Recommendation Generation
    • Conversational and Interactive Recommendations
  • Software and AI Integration
    • Automating Key Development Processes, including: 
      • Code Generation, Automated Debugging, and Code Optimization
    • Improving Code Quality and Maintainability
    • Accelerating the Software Development Lifecycle
    • Addressing the challenges of deploying and scaling AI-powered software applications
  • Revolutionizing Information Retrieval
    • Enhancing Query Understanding
    • Personalized Information Synthesis
    • Advanced Ranking and Filtering
    • Multimodal Information Retrieval
  • Enhancing Reproducibility in Scientific Research
    • Automating Experiment Tracking and Logging
    • Enhancing Data Management and Sharing
    • Developing Standardized Evaluation Benchmarks
    • Improving Research Communication and Dissemination
  • Advancing RAG Systems
    • Improving Retrieval Efficiency and Accuracy
    • Enhancing Contextual Understanding
    • Improving Generation Quality
  • Evaluation and Ethical Considerations
    • Evaluating Effectiveness of LLM-based agents in different scenarios
    • Addressing Ethical Challenges including Hallucination and Bias

Why Attend?

  • Stay Ahead of AI Trends – Gain insights into the latest advancements in LLM-based agents.
  • Engage with Leading Experts – Participate in keynotes and discussions with researchers and industry professionals.
  • Showcase & Collaborate – Present your research, explore potential collaborations, and contribute to AI-driven innovation.

Join us at LLAIS 2025 to shape the future of intelligent, adaptable, and ethical AI systems!
